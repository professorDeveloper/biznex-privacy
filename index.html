Biznex Back Office is the mobile management companion for restaurant and retail businesses on the Biznex Cloud platform. Owners and branch managers use it to monitor operations from anywhere. We collect only what the app needs to work.

In short: we process your account and business data to run the app, keep diagnostics to stay reliable, and never sell your data or use it for advertising.
Section 1

Data we collect

We collect three categories of data, all in service of running the app for your business:

Account data

Name, email, phone and role — used to authenticate you and control access. Accounts are created by your business administrator.

Business data

Sales, orders, inventory, staff and financial records that you and your business generate within the platform.

Technical data

App version, device type and diagnostic logs — used to keep the service secure and reliable.

Section 2

How we use it

We use your data solely to provide and secure the service. Specifically:

  • Authenticating you and enforcing role-based access.
  • Displaying your business reports, dashboards and operations.
  • Diagnosing issues and keeping the app stable.

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or track you across other apps or websites.

Section 3

Storage & security

Authentication tokens are stored encrypted on your device. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections and stored on secured servers.

Section 4

Data sharing

Data is shared only within your own organization, according to your assigned role and permissions. We do not share personal data with third parties except as required by law.

Section 5

Retention

We retain business and account data for as long as your account is active, and as required by applicable law.

Section 6

Your rights

You may request access to or deletion of your account data by contacting your business administrator or us at the address below.
