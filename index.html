Privacy Policy

Biznex Back Office

Last updated: 2 July 2026

Biznex Back Office (“the App”) is a business management application for merchants using the Biznex Cloud POS platform. This policy explains what data we process and why.

1. Data we collect

  • Account data: name, email, phone number, and role, used to authenticate you and control access. Accounts are created by your business administrator.
  • Business data: sales, orders, inventory, staff, and financial records that you and your business generate within the platform.
  • Technical data: app version, device type, and diagnostic logs, used to keep the service secure and reliable.

2. How we use data

We use your data only to provide and secure the service: authentication, displaying your business reports and operations, and troubleshooting. We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or track you across other apps or websites.

3. Data storage and security

Authentication tokens are stored encrypted on your device. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections and stored on secured servers.

4. Data sharing

Data is shared only within your own organization according to your assigned role and permissions. We do not share personal data with third parties except as required by law.

5. Data retention

We retain business and account data for as long as your account is active, and as required by applicable law.

6. Your rights

You may request access to or deletion of your account data by contacting your business administrator or us at the address below.
