Biznex Kassir is a point-of-sale terminal used by staff of restaurant and retail businesses. It is offline-first: it talks to an on-site Biznex server over the local network, which is the single bridge to the cloud. We process only what running the till requires — no advertising, no tracking.

01 Data we process

Staff account data

Employee name, role and PIN — used to sign in to the terminal and control access. Accounts are created by your administrator; there is no public sign-up.

Operational data

Orders, menu items, tables, cashier shifts and payment records generated during work — stored on the device and synced through the on-site server.

Diagnostic data

App version, device model and crash logs (via Sentry) — used only to keep the app stable. Not linked to your identity, never used for tracking.

02 Device permissions

Camera

Used only to scan the server connection QR code. No photos are taken or stored.

Local Network

Used to discover the on-site POS server on the same Wi-Fi (Bonjour / mDNS). The app does not scan or track other devices.

03 How we use it

Data is used solely to run the point of sale: authenticating staff, taking orders and payments, printing receipts, and keeping the terminal in sync with the business server. We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or track you across other apps or websites.

04 Storage & sharing

Business data is stored on the device and on the business’s own on-site server, shared only within your organization according to your role, and transmitted over encrypted connections. We do not share personal data with third parties except as required by law. Diagnostic data is processed by our error-monitoring provider (Sentry) on our behalf.

05 Retention

Operational and account data is retained for as long as your account and business remain active, and as required by applicable law and tax regulations.

06 Your rights

You may request access to or deletion of your account data by contacting your business administrator or us at the address below.
