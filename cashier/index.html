Privacy Policy

Biznex Kassir — Point of Sale

Last updated: 2 July 2026

Biznex Kassir (“the App”) is a point-of-sale (POS) terminal used by staff of restaurant and retail businesses on the Biznex Cloud platform. It is offline-first: it connects to an on-site Biznex server over the local network, which is the single bridge to the cloud. This policy explains what data the App processes and why.

1. Data we process

  • Staff account data: employee name, role, and PIN, used to sign in to the terminal and control access. Accounts are created by your business administrator; the App has no public sign-up.
  • Operational data: orders, menu items, tables, cashier shifts and payment records that staff generate during work. This is business data, stored locally on the device and synchronised through the on-site server.
  • Diagnostic data: app version, device model and crash/error logs (via Sentry) used only to keep the App stable. This is not linked to your identity and is not used for tracking or advertising.

2. Device permissions

  • Camera — used only to scan the server connection QR code. No photos are taken or stored.
  • Local Network — used to discover the on-site POS server on the same Wi-Fi network (Bonjour/mDNS). The App does not scan or track other devices.

3. How we use data

Data is used solely to operate the point of sale: authenticating staff, taking orders and payments, printing receipts, and keeping the terminal in sync with the business server. We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or track you across other apps or websites.

4. Data storage and sharing

Business data is stored on the device and on the business’s own on-site server, and is shared only within your organisation according to your role. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections. We do not share personal data with third parties except as required by law. Diagnostic data is processed by our error-monitoring provider (Sentry) on our behalf.

5. Data retention

Operational and account data is retained for as long as your account and business remain active, and as required by applicable law and tax regulations.

6. Your rights

You may request access to or deletion of your account data by contacting your business administrator or us at the address below.
